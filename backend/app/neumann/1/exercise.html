Think


The following chart shows what happens when you put $10,000 into different money jars and let them grow for 10 years. Each line is a different way to save or invest money.
Try to understand this chart and think about the following:

- Which line would you want your money to follow? Why?”
- Would you rather have a bumpy ride that ends with more money, or a smooth ride with less money?
- Why do you think saving money doesn’t grow as fast as the other lines?
- What do you think inflation means if money can buy less over time?
